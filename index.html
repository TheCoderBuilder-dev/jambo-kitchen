Our Story

A Taste of Home, Served with Love

Since 2014, Jambo Kitchen has been serving the heart and soul of Kenyan cuisine to families across Nairobi.

What started as a small family eatery in the vibrant streets of Eastleigh has grown into one of Nairobi's most beloved casual dining destinations. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic taste that makes Kenyan food so special.
